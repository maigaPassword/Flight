What is Budget Buy?

Budget Buy is Skyvela's intelligent price alert system that monitors flight prices 24/7 and notifies you when fares drop within your budget. Instead of constantly checking prices, set your maximum budget and let us do the work for you.

How Budget Buy Works

1

Set Your Budget & Route

Enter your departure city, destination, travel dates, and most importantly - your maximum budget. Be realistic but flexible with dates for best results.

2

We Monitor Prices

Our advanced system continuously tracks flight prices from hundreds of airlines in real-time, looking for fares that match your budget criteria.

3

Get Instant Alerts

When we find a flight within your budget, you'll receive an instant email and in-app notification with all the details.

4

Book Quickly

Great deals don't last long! Click the link in your alert to review and book the flight before prices change.

Tips for Getting the Best Deals

Be Flexible with Dates

Mid-week flights (Tuesday-Thursday) are often cheaper. Use the flexible date option.

Set Realistic Budgets

Research average prices for your route. Setting budgets too low may result in no matches.

Book Early

Create your Budget Buy request 2-3 months in advance for international flights.

Act Quickly

When you receive an alert, book fast! Prices can change within hours.

Consider Nearby Airports

Flying to/from alternative airports can sometimes save hundreds of dollars.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Budget Buy free to use?

Yes! Budget Buy is completely free. You only pay the flight price when you book.

How many Budget Buy requests can I create?

There's no limit! Create as many Budget Buy requests as you need for different routes and dates.

What if I never get an alert?

If your budget is too low or dates are inflexible, you may not receive alerts. Try increasing your budget or adding date flexibility.

How quickly do I need to book after receiving an alert?

Flight prices can change within hours or even minutes. We recommend booking as soon as possible after receiving an alert.

Can I use Budget Buy for international flights?

Absolutely! Budget Buy works for both domestic and international flights from hundreds of airlines worldwide.
