Important Payment Information

Currency

All prices are displayed in USD. Your card issuer may apply currency conversion fees for international cards.

Payment Timing

Full payment is required at the time of booking. Your card will be charged immediately upon confirmation.

Card Verification

For security, we may require additional verification (CVV, 3D Secure, billing address match).

Billing Descriptor

Your card statement will show "SKYVELA - FLIGHT BOOKING" along with your booking reference.

Failed Payments

If payment fails, check your card details and available credit. Contact your bank if issues persist.

Refunds & Additional Charges

Refund Processing
