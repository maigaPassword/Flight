Cancellation Policy

Understand your options and rights

Overview

At Skyvela, we understand that plans can change. Our cancellation policy is designed to be fair and transparent. Please review the terms below to understand your options.

Important: Cancellation policies may vary by airline, fare type, and route. Always check your specific booking terms before purchasing.

24-Hour Risk-Free Cancellation

Most bookings made on Skyvela can be cancelled within 24 hours of purchase for a full refund, provided the booking was made at least 7 days before departure.

  • • Full refund to original payment method
  • • No cancellation fees
  • • Applies to most fare types
  • • Must be within 24 hours of booking confirmation

Standard Cancellation Terms

✓ Refundable Tickets

  • • Can be cancelled anytime before departure
  • • Refund amount depends on airline policy
  • • May incur airline cancellation fees ($100-$500)
  • • Refund processed within 7-20 business days
  • • Original payment method will be credited

✗ Non-Refundable Tickets

  • • Generally cannot be cancelled for a refund
  • • May be eligible for travel credit/voucher (minus fees)
  • • Change fees typically apply ($200-$750 per ticket)
  • • Fare difference must be paid for rebooking
  • • Travel credit usually valid for 12 months

⚠ Basic Economy Fares

  • • Most restrictive fare type
  • • Usually non-refundable and non-changeable
  • • No refund or credit available after 24-hour window
  • • Check specific airline policy before booking

How to Cancel Your Booking

1.

Log in to your Skyvela account

2.

Go to "My Bookings"

3.

Select the booking you want to cancel

4.

Click "Cancel Booking" and follow the prompts

5.

Review cancellation fees and refund amount

6.

Confirm cancellation and receive confirmation email

Special Circumstances

Medical Emergencies

With proper documentation (doctor's note), some airlines may waive cancellation fees or offer full refunds.

Flight Cancellations by Airline

If the airline cancels your flight, you are entitled to a full refund regardless of fare type.

Travel Insurance

Consider purchasing travel insurance for maximum flexibility and protection against unforeseen events.

Natural Disasters or Pandemics

Airlines may offer waivers during extraordinary circumstances. Check with the specific airline.

Important Notes

  • Refund processing times vary by airline and payment method (typically 7-20 business days)
  • Cancellation policies are subject to change based on airline terms and conditions
  • Always review your booking confirmation email for specific cancellation terms
  • Group bookings (10+ passengers) may have different cancellation terms
